## Post Text

A Jaipur coaching institute owner told me last month: "I know AI can help. I just don't know where to start."

He runs 3 batches a day. 200+ students. His staff spends 4 hours every evening answering parents on WhatsApp — fee queries, attendance updates, schedule changes. The same 15 questions, over and over.

He'd tried ChatGPT. Used it to draft some marketing messages. That was his "AI adoption."

NASSCOM says 72% of Indian MSMEs need AI training. I think that understates it. They don't need training on how AI works. They need someone to set it up and make it work on WhatsApp, where their customers already are.

We built him an AI employee in under 12 hours.

It answers parent queries in Hindi and English. Sends fee reminders on the 1st and 15th. Shares attendance updates after every batch. Routes complex complaints to the owner directly.

The result: his staff got 4 hours back. Every single day.

Harvard's RCT on AI tutoring showed doubled learning gains. MagicSchool AI saves teachers 7+ hours weekly. The data is clear — education and AI aren't just compatible, they multiply each other.

But a Jaipur coaching institute doesn't need a Harvard study. It needs fewer WhatsApp messages at 10 PM.

We broke two integrations before getting the Hindi responses right. Third time worked.

What repetitive task is your team drowning in? Genuinely curious — we're mapping every use case we hear.
