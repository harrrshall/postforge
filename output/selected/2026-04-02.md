## Post Text

78M Indian MSMEs. Under 5% use AI agents. Here's a 5-step framework to go from "I use ChatGPT" to "AI runs my operations."

Most Indian SMBs think they're "using AI" because they paste text into ChatGPT. That's Level 1. Here's the full ladder:

Step 1: Identify Your Time Drain
Find the one task your team repeats 20+ times daily. For clinics, it's WhatsApp patient queries. For coaching centers, it's fee reminders. For e-commerce, it's order status updates. Pick ONE.

Step 2: Map the Conversation
Document every question customers ask about that task. A Pune clinic found 12 recurring WhatsApp questions covered 85% of all inbound messages.

Step 3: Build on WhatsApp First
Not email. Not a website widget. WhatsApp. 78% of Indian business communication happens there. Meet customers where they already are.

Step 4: Go Hybrid from Day 1
Klarna saved $60M with AI, then rehired humans. The production-proven model is 60-70% AI, 30-40% human. Route complex issues to a real person automatically.

Step 5: Measure in Rupees, Not "Efficiency"
14 minutes saved per clinician per day = 2 extra patients = ₹1,000/day = ₹30,000/month. Your AI employee costs ₹4,999. If the math doesn't work in ₹, it doesn't work.

The gap between "I use ChatGPT" and "AI handles my operations" is exactly 5 steps.

Which step is your business stuck on? Drop your industry and I'll tell you what Step 1 looks like for you.
