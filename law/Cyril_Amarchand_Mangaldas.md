## 1. Firm Overview

| Field | Details |
|---|---|
| **Full Name** | Cyril Amarchand Mangaldas (CAM) |
| **Founded** | May 11, 2015 (from the split of Amarchand & Mangaldas & Suresh A Shroff & Co) |
| **Managing Partner** | Cyril Shroff |
| **Headquarters** | Mumbai, India |
| **Total Lawyers** | ~1,200 (as of July 2025) |
| **Total Partners** | ~220 |
| **Total Employees** | ~1,700 (including non-legal staff) |
| **Website** | [cyrilshroff.com](https://www.cyrilshroff.com/) |
| **Core Values** | The 3Cs — Character, Competence, Commitment |
| **Type** | Full-service law firm (general practice) |

CAM is India's largest full-service law firm and one of the most prominent legal practices in Asia. It was created from the demerger of the legacy firm Amarchand & Mangaldas & Suresh A Shroff & Co, with Cyril Shroff retaining the Mumbai headquarters and the majority of the corporate/M&A practice. The Delhi-based practice went to Shardul Shroff (now Shardul Amarchand Mangaldas).

## 7. Work Processes & Client Engagement

### 7.1 Client Engagement Model

- **Partner-led teams:** Every major client has a dedicated partner who leads the engagement
- **Engagement letters** with defined scope, conflict checks, and KYC/AML compliance
- **Secondments:** Lawyers deployed to client premises for extended periods
- **Client-centric approach:** Focus on cost-effectiveness, time-efficiency, transparency

### 7.2 Deal Execution Process (M&A Example)

```
1. Client Intake & Conflict Check
2. Engagement Letter & Scope Definition
3. Due Diligence (AI-assisted document review)
4. Data Room Management
5. Negotiation & Drafting
6. Regulatory Filing Coordination (SEBI, CCI, RBI, etc.)
7. Closing & Post-Closing Compliance
```

### 7.3 Dispute Resolution Workflow

- Advisory → Pre-litigation strategy → Filing → Court appearances (High Courts, Supreme Court)
- International arbitration (ICC, SIAC, LCIA, Maxwell Chambers)
- Emphasis on Alternative Dispute Resolution (ADR) to minimize time and costs

### 7.4 Quality Assurance

- Hierarchical review: Associate → Senior Associate → Principal Associate → Partner
- Document automation for standardization
- AI-powered proofreading and error detection
- Internal knowledge management portals with regular practice group updates

### 7.5 Billing Structure

- Specific fee arrangements are not publicly disclosed
- Industry sources indicate the firm uses a combination of hourly billing, fixed fees, and retainer arrangements depending on matter type
- Known for premium pricing consistent with its Tier-1 market position

## 13. Controversies & Complaints

### 13.1 The 2015 Split

The most significant public controversy was the split of Amarchand & Mangaldas & Suresh A Shroff & Co into two firms in 2015 following a family dispute between brothers Cyril Shroff and Shardul Shroff. The matter was resolved through arbitration/negotiation:
- **Cyril Shroff** retained the Mumbai practice → Cyril Amarchand Mangaldas (CAM)
- **Shardul Shroff** established the Delhi practice → Shardul Amarchand Mangaldas (SAM)

### 13.2 Work Culture Concerns

- Anonymous complaints on Glassdoor about long hours and high-pressure culture (consistent with industry norms for elite Indian law firms)
- Broader industry discussions about junior lawyer compensation and working conditions sometimes reference CAM

### 13.3 No Major Regulatory Actions

- No publicly reported bar council disciplinary actions or sanctions against the firm
- No major client complaints or malpractice issues in public records

### 13.4 Public Commentary on High-Profile Matters

- The firm's involvement in high-profile matters (Tata-Mistry dispute, Vodafone-Idea, Adani-related work, insolvency cases) occasionally generates public commentary, but this is standard for firms handling contentious matters
