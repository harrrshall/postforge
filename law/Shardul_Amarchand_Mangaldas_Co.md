## Client Complaints & Criticism

### Employee Reviews (Glassdoor / AmbitionBox Themes)

**Positives:**
- Excellent learning opportunities and exposure to high-quality work
- Strong brand name and career credibility
- Strong deal flow and complex matter exposure
- Good for early-career lawyers building skills

**Negatives:**
- Long working hours (common across all top-tier Indian law firms)
- High pressure and demanding workloads
- Work-life balance concerns
- Hierarchical culture
- These complaints are broadly similar to peer firms (AZB, CAM, Khaitan, Trilegal)

### Notable Controversies

**The 2015 Split (Major):**
The biggest public controversy was the split of the legacy Amarchand & Mangaldas & Suresh A Shroff & Co in 2015. The separation between Shardul Shroff and Cyril Shroff divided the firm along geographic lines (North/South vs. Mumbai). There were public reports of disputes over the firm's legacy, client relationships, and brand name.

**Partner Exits:**
As with all top-tier firms, SAM experiences regular lateral partner movement. The firm has both gained and lost partners over the years. Notable departures and arrivals are regularly reported by legal media.

### Bar Council / Disciplinary Actions
No publicly reported formal Bar Council of India disciplinary actions against SAM as a firm. (BCI proceedings are not always widely reported.)
