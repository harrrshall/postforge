## 2. Technology Tools & Infrastructure

### 2.1 AI & Legal Technology

**Harvey AI (Generative AI Platform)**
- SAM is the FIRST Indian law firm to implement Harvey AI's full suite across all 7 offices
- Announced after a year-long evaluation of both Indian and international AI solutions (including exploring building their own)
- Use cases:
  - Contract drafting and review acceleration
  - Due diligence process streamlining
  - Legal research and predictive analysis
  - Data-driven insights for contentious and advisory matters
  - Drafting complex clauses
  - Creating closing checklists
  - Generating client pitches
- Governance: Dedicated AI task force (Partners Naval Chopra, VR Neelakantan, Hemant Krishna, and CDIO Irshad Saifi)
- Safeguards: Firm-specific data security measures, prompt-engineering standards, continuous human oversight for client privilege and regulatory compliance

**AI/ML Automation Suite**
- Processing complex legal documents
- Contract review capabilities
- Document comparison
- Proofreading tools

### 2.2 Infrastructure & Networking

**Citrix Digital Workspace**
- Citrix SD-WAN deployed across all branches
- Citrix Endpoint Management for BYOD security
- Citrix XenApp for virtualized application delivery
- Consolidated multiple MPLS links with real-time failover
- Centralized visibility and management via single-pane monitoring
- Enables work-from-anywhere on any device

**Riverbed SteelHead**
- SteelHead for data center-to-branch optimization
- SteelHead Mobile for remote/mobile user optimization
- Results: 20-30% improvement in Citrix XenApp performance
- 70-80% faster data replication between data center and DR site
- Significantly reduced bandwidth consumption and costs

## 3. Work Processes & Methodologies

### 3.1 General Approach
- **Multidisciplinary teams** with global exposure and deep sector knowledge
- **Cross-practice collaboration** - breaking down barriers between divisions for seamless service delivery
- **Selective client strategy** - "concentrate on fewer clients and doing deeper work with them" to give more attention
- **Relationship-driven** - personal relationships with clients cited as a key differentiator
- Technical expertise merged with interpersonal skills for practical solutions

### 3.2 M&A / Corporate Practice
- Full-spectrum advisory: mergers, acquisitions, demergers, delistings, public M&A, asset transfers, joint ventures, restructurings, amalgamations
- Cross-border transaction capability through Lex Mundi network
- Multi-jurisdictional client base
- Integrated approach combining corporate, tax, competition, and regulatory expertise

### 3.3 Dispute Resolution / Litigation
- 130+ dispute lawyers including 38 partners
- Led by Managing Partner Pallavi Shroff
- Strategy-first methodology: "cutting edge planning" translated into "practical solutions that synchronise with the objectives of the client at every stage"
- Seamless collaboration across General Corporate, Competition Law, Tax, and IP practices
- Comprehensive risk assessment and compliance program development to reduce litigation exposure
- Covers: Supreme Court, High Courts, tribunals, and all major arbitration institutions (ICC, LCIA, SIAC, HKIAC, DIAC, ICA, ICADR)
- Specialized in shareholder disputes, white collar crime, internal investigations, FCPA/UKBA compliance

### 3.4 Competition Law
- Full spectrum: merger reviews, abuse of dominance, leniency applications
- Known for geographic coverage breadth and deep personnel strength

- Designed, implemented, and overseen by dedicated Learning & Development (L&D) Team
- Led by Chandan Shrivastav (Head - L&D), formerly at Accenture, BCG, and Genpact
- Covers associates from the stage they join

### 5.2 CLEmentine - AI-Powered E-Learning Platform
- Cloud-based, AI-enabled learning management system
- Replaced traditional classroom teaching with virtual platform
- Features:
  - 200+ courses created by senior lawyers and partners
  - AI-powered personalized learning paths
  - Automatic content tagging for customized search results
  - Interactive content delivery
  - Cross-platform: smartphones, tablets, PCs, laptops
  - 24/7 video training access
  - Progress tracking dashboards
- Course topics: dispute resolution, corporate foundations, financial statement analysis, writing skills, presentation skills, SAM Dialogue sessions on trends
