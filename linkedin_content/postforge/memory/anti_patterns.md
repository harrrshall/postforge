## Known Anti-Patterns (from research, pre-data)

### 1. Generic AI Language
- "In today's fast-paced world" → 360Brew flags as AI-slop
- "Let me break it down" → Detected as generic pattern
- "Game-changer" / "Revolutionary" → Empty superlatives

### 2. Weak Hooks
- Starting with "I'm excited to share..." → No curiosity gap
- Starting with a question without stakes → No reason to click "See more"
- Hook longer than 150 chars → Truncated before the punch

### 3. Engagement Bait
- "Comment YES if you agree" → Algorithm penalizes
- "Tag someone who needs this" → Detected as bait
- "Repost this" without adding value → Blind repost is invisible

### 4. External Links
- Any URL in post body → 60% reach penalty
- "Link in first comment" → Also penalized in 2026

### 5. Off-Topic Posts
- Posting about unrelated topics → Topic DNA dilution
- Personal content > 5% of total → Expertise signal weakens
